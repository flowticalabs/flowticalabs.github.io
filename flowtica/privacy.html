Flowtica Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

Flowtica respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information Flowtica collects, how it is used, and your rights when using the app.

By using Flowtica, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Flowtica is designed to work without requiring an account or sign-in.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, payment information, messages, or personal files.

App Usage Analytics

Flowtica uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and improve stability and features. Firebase Analytics may collect device type, operating system version, app version, country or general region, screens viewed, session duration, crashes or performance issues, and anonymous usage events. This information is aggregated and does not directly identify you.

Local App Data

Flowtica stores your focus sessions, streaks, categories, plant progress, themes, and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you manually back up or transfer your phone.

2. Permissions Used

Flowtica only requests vibration permission to vibrate the device when a focus session ends or important actions occur.

Flowtica does not request access to camera, microphone, contacts, location, notifications, storage, or files.

3. How We Use Information

We use analytics data only to improve app performance, fix crashes and bugs, understand which features are useful, and improve the overall user experience.

We do not sell your data, share your personal data with advertisers, or use your information for targeted advertising. Flowtica does not include ads, ad tracking, or third-party advertising SDKs.

4. Third-Party Services

Flowtica uses Firebase Analytics by Google. Firebase may process anonymous technical data according to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

Analytics data collected by Firebase may be retained according to Google’s standard retention policies.

All app data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete the app, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Flowtica is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Because most Flowtica data is stored only on your device, you remain in control of your information.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what information is collected
  • The right to request deletion of information
  • The right to opt out of analytics collection through your device settings

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page.
